# Reference Organization

Reference PDFs are grouped by provisional relevance to the current thesis
framing: emissions/carbon-footprint comparison between road-only freight and
road-cabotage-road multimodal freight in Brazil.

## Folders

- `core/`: papers likely central to the thesis argument, comparison method, or
  emissions model, including Brazilian cabotage, short sea shipping vs road,
  port hoteling, and at-berth emissions.
- `support/`: useful references for background, method robustness, MRV/LCA
  framing, terminal equipment, external costs, or broader multimodal emissions.
- `low-use/`: references with a weaker connection to the current thesis focus.
  Low-use does not mean useless; it means less central under the current scope.

Classification is intentionally conservative and provisional. If a paper may
support emissions methodology, it should stay in `support/` rather than
`low-use/` until the final thesis boundary is settled.
